Common questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
PouchWise is one subscription, managed by Apple. Open the Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → PouchWise, or go to the “Subscription” screen inside the app. Cancelling there stops future renewals; deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?
Open the app → You → Subscription → “Restore purchases.”

Where is my data stored?
On your device. PouchWise has no account and doesn't upload your logs. See our Privacy Policy.

How do I delete my data?
Settings → Data & privacy → “Delete all my data,” or simply delete the app.
